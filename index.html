THE HISTORY OF POKEMON

Pokemon: red and green

The first ever pokemon game was pokemon: red and green, created by Satoshi Tajiri. It was released first in Japan, 1996. This game would be the start of a long series. This game fetured Ash, a pokemon hunter, who captured pokemon to use in battles. He especially liked to battle with his arch nemisis Gary Oakes, with his best friend- the pokemon Pikachu, who would go on to feature in his own game later.

Pokemon: blue - special edition

Pokemon: blue - special edition is a special edition game of pokemon red and green. It is much the same, the only difference is the new pokemon, new levels and new storyline. This game was especially popular in the amricas.

Pokemon: Yellow - special Piachu edition

This game is considered to be one of the funnest games in pokemon history. It, hence it's name, is a game that features pikachu, and his buds. This game is recomended for many people who play to have a good time.

Pokemon: Gold and Silver

Like pokemon red and green, blue, and yellow, pokemon gold and silver is played from a third person point of view, walking the land of pokemon. The game starts when the player has a choice between the gold or silver pokemon. You face many dangers including team rocket, and the anonymous theif who stole one of proffesser Elm's pokemon. Pokemon gold and silver progressed the game of pokemon further.
